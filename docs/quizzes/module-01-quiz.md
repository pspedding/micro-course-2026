## Question 1

*Lesson L01 · Scarcity Choice*


What is the fundamental economic problem?


**Type:** Multiple Choice

- **A)** Unlimited wants vs. limited resources
- **B)** Government intervention in markets
- **C)** Technological advancement
- **D)** Global trade imbalances

??? success "Show Answer"
    **Answer: A) Unlimited wants vs. limited resources**

    Economics studies how societies allocate scarce resources to satisfy unlimited wants.

## Question 2

## Question 8
